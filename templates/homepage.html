About this Project

As someone who uses ethereum on a weekly basis and a long time hodler, I wanted to be able to view the ethereum in my wallet and see how my balance changed over time, as sort of a progress tracker. I found that ethereum data is plentiful and easily accessible, so I wanted to create a one stop destination where anybody can view the data of any wallet, without needing to connect via web3. Besides just viewing my balance, I thought that there should be better ways to visualize the data for my own personal trades and have always wondered which wallets I have sent ethereum to and how frequently.
